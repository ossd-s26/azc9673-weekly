# Week 6 Blog: Major Project Expectations and Contributions

For this class' major group assignment that we are all about to begin, I am most interested in contributing to a project that incorporates my interests, whether that be in my career: AI and fintech, or in my free time: video games, music, and movies. My goals for the rest of this class will be to make meaningful contributions to such a project that I can be proud of, that genuinely improve the project in some way, and that allow me to learn and develop as a programmer.
